Our Mission

Why Choose ILMA Quran Academy?

ILMA Quran Academy was built to make Quran learning simple, personal, and accessible for every Muslim worldwide.

Students learn 1-on-1 with certified teachers, at flexible timings, in a warm and supportive environment rooted in Islamic values.

From your first Arabic letter to advanced Tajweed and Hifz, we guide your journey with care, patience, and expertise.

This isn’t just online learning — it’s a trusted path to connect with the Quran.

Thousands of families rely on ILMA Quran Academy. You can too.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a trial class?

Simply fill out the form below or message us on WhatsApp. Our coordinator will schedule a time that suits your timezone.

Are fees refundable?

No, fees are non-refundable once the month has started. However, you can cancel before the new month begins.

Do you have female tutors?

Yes, we have highly qualified female tutors available for female students and children.

Is it a video or audio call?

Classes are conducted via Skype/Zoom/WhatsApp using Screen Sharing. Video is optional based on student comfort.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal, Western Union, Bank Transfer, and other major remittance services.
